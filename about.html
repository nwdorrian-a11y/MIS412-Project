My Hometown

I am from Fredericksburg, Virginia, located between Richmond and Fairfax along the Rappahannock River. I spent most of my childhood there, from the age of five until I moved away to college when I was eighteen.

Fredericksburg is great, if I had not grown up there, it is the type of area where I could see myself spending the rest of my life.
